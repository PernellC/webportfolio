Who is Pernell?

My Story

I grew up in NYC and I wouldn’t change it for anything. What borough you ask? “Show love it’s the Brooklyn way!”

When I’m not working on a project, I am usually checking out that new restaurant or bar. Living in NYC there is always something new for you to explore. During the colder months I hit the mountains to snowboard, however I'v been a beginner forever.

Since I was young, I thought I would end up doing something creative. I used to spend hours and hours tinkering with photoshop when I was younger (CS3 days). I took a long detour after getting stuck in the wrong major in high school (electrical instead of graphic design). Nonetheless I’m back to reclaim my time and flex my creative chops.

I worked in the wealth management industry for 5 years developing strategies for various planning situations. I focused a lot on the planning software and how advisors can utilize the platform to illustrate what they were trying to convey to their clients. Through planning I gained a lot of useful financial industry insights and would like to work to make it a more user-friendly place. You often see flashy commercials for financial institutions throwing all their weight behind their brand. Brand recognition will get people in the door, but once they’re there I would like to make sure the experience keeps them there.
