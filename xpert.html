Xpert

Overview – The app’s mission is to provide access to an expert in any field and subsequently connecting them with the right expert for the task at hand.

Objective – The goal is to find the user an answer or solution to any problem that they may be looking for the answer for.

Role – UX Designer

Duration – 9 months

Process – Since I created Xpert from the ground up I utilized a 4 step UX design process. The 4 steps I took while creating the app were user research, design, testing and implementation.

During the user research phase I received a lot of feedback from users as to what they would like in an app to find experts in addition to what competitors have on the market. From here I created 2 user personas that embodied the ideal user of the app.

As I approached the design phase I went for a cheerful, welcoming, and easy to use design. During user testing I realized a lot of my missteps. A lot of the feedback had to do with odd or unfamiliar placement of elements along with poor contrast in certain sections of the app.

In conclusion I ended up with a design that was more familiar to users and had higher contrast throughout the app. I was very happy with what I produced for users to test and their feedback. The main theme was do not reinvent the wheel, which led to a design that was clean and straightforward.
